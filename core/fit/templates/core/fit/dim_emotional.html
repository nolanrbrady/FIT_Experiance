The Emotional Dimension
Ilene Rusk, PhD
Emotional DIMENSION COACH

Feelings of peace and joy are expressions of health. We typically focus on the physical body when we refer to health, but the state of our minds, our hearts, and our relationships are connected and directly contribute to our physical health, our ability to make good decisions, and our overall sense of wellbeing. At FIT we believe that each person can flourish Cognitively, Emotionally, Physically, and Financially when we begin to understand how our minds and brains are bonded.

FIT Emotional is based upon a positive neuroscience-based model. There is a wealth of brain-based evidence indicating you have the capacity to develop spirituality and positivity and to experience an abundant life. Our mission here at FIT is to link you with specific techniques and skills that will enhance your daily functioning so you can access your best self and express positivity at work and in your social relationships. This allows you to say yes to a more joyful, productive, and integrated you.

Taking the FIT assessment will give you a clear understanding of your Emotional Health, Emotional Intelligence, Stress Resilience, Gratitude and Positivity, Spirituality, and Social Engagement.
