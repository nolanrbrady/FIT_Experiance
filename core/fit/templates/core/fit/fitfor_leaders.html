You are always expected to lead at the highest level with the most critical decisions that have the greatest risks or rewards. As a result of your time and energy, we know you are pulled in multiple and sometimes opposite directions. Your challenges are unique and complex.

The vitality of an organization depends on the leadership. Thus, it is essential to have FIT leaders to run FIT organizations. We know that there are many times when you are the most powerful person in the room when it comes to the final decision to be made. We also know if you are not Cognitively, Emotionally, Physically, and Financially FIT, your decisions are clouded, and the results could be costly. Further, we believe that a healthy balance in life will result in a consistently greater leadership output and enjoyment in all facets of life. Unfortunately, this battle for a healthy balance is often lost amongst influential leaders. However, you have the opportunity to become FIT.

The FIT process is a holistic approach that enables you to identify where you are (Current State), determine where you want to be (Desired Future State), and provide a pathway to achieving your dreams (Strategic Process).

Current State

Becoming FIT begins by identifying who and where you are (your current state). By completing our comprehensive FIT Assessment, you will receive a clear inventory across four key life dimensions: Cognitive, Emotional, Physical, and Financial. As a result of this assessment, you will know how integrated, aligned, and balanced you are across these dimensions, which will enable you to effectively engage in the right areas for improvement. Research suggests that three of the main characteristics employees are looking for in a leader are trust, compassion, and stability. By assessing your Current State, we can calculate your greatest assets and areas that need attention.

Future Desired State

After analyzing your assessment results, FIT assists you in identifying your Future Desired State. This includes a process of identifying your goals and areas that are lacking fulfillment. We will prompt you to consider whether there are aspects of your life that you want to change or improve. Identifying your Future Desired State includes recognizing the obstacles that are keeping you from achieving what you most desire in life. In addition to identifying your personal Future Desired State, FIT facilitates this process with your team members. Fully Integrated Teams are aligned in their sense of purpose and identity. A FIT leader will seek to understand team members’ interests and dreams. When executives and employees feel a sense of common ground, both parties are more likely to show adaptive behaviors to help the company achieve their goals.

Strategic Process

Finally, FIT helps you develop your Strategic Process, bridging the gap between Current and Desired Future States. This plan will help you identify and remove barriers in your life, such as beliefs about what is possible, and guide you toward your Desired Self. The FIT process involves helping executives define and fine-tune their purpose, which has been shown to have nonpecuniary benefits as well as economically favorable outcomes. Productivity increases, and costs decrease when the executive leaders are operationally FIT.

No one can get to where they want to go alone. It can be lonely at the top. The greatest leaders know when to ask for help. We are here to help you become a transformational and authentic leader.

Leading intentionally starts with building positive relationships. We look forward to building a relationship with you as you become FIT in 4D.
