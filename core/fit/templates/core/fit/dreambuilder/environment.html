Your Environment



RECOGNIZE YOUR ENVIRONMENT

When building a home, selecting the lot is very important. Your environment is made up of the people, resources and places of which you interact and live. It is important to identify those who are nutrient rich and those which are toxic to your purpose and your dreams. What is disabling you from building a FIT House? What people are enabling you to achieve your purpose? Is your environment nutrient rich for your purpose to be cultivated and realized? Who brings energy into your life? Adversely, who sucks the energy from you?
