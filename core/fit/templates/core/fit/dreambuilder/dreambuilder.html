Build Your Dream

Getting Started

Now that you have identified your current state through the FIT Assessment, it is time to create your desired future state. In other words, it is time to build your custom dream house or what we refer to as your FIT house.


Your Fit House

Building a home requires the right integration of resources and steps. You will be guided through the essential steps to create your custom FIT house.


Next steps

1
 Environment Here you will identify the nutrients and toxins in your current environment. These include people, resources, and things.
2
 Purpose In this step you are building your foundation. You will be guided to create a one breath purpose statement that answers succinctly why you exist.
3
 Principles Building off of your foundation, - your purpose, you will now create the fundamental truths that guide your thoughts and behaviors. These principles are non-negotiables that will withstand any test.
4
 Vision With the above identified, now you will create a clear and compelling vision for your life in the next 1-5 years.
5
 Dream Document Print out your specific, custom built FIT House to keep in front of you each day as you move towards a more optimal FIT life.
