Getting Started

Whether you have built your FIT house, or if you just want to start working on improving individual dimensions and sub-categories, we want you to engage in the Plan Builder. Here you can select up to four sub categories to work on by picking goals within each sub-category. That doesnâ€™t mean you have to select four, you may only choose to select one, but no more than four. These goals or sub categories can be within one dimension or one in each of the four dimensions. After the goals have been selected, pick up to two tasks in each, for a maximum of eight tasks. We ask to limit the amount of tasks you can work on within a week so not to overwhelm you and allow proper focus on the tasks you have selected. Once the week has finished, go back and select new tasks, or stay with the same tasks. We suggest you let your CIRCUMFIT guide your selection of goals and tasks.

These goals and tasks have been specifically designed to help you grow within each sub - category and dimension.Changing habits is hard and requires dedication and patience.Following the plan will help you develop new habits which will improve your FIT score as well as your quality of life.Effort and focus will help you move from where you are to where you want to be.
