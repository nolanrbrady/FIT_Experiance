The Cognitive Dimension
Rena Kirkland, PhD
Cognitive DIMENSION COACH

Cognitive FITness is vital to every aspect of your life. Your thinking patterns are related to your emotions, physical health, and financial decisions. Your thoughts impact the quality of your social interactions, what you eat, and how you approach your goals. The FIT process encompasses learning about your current thinking habits and engaging in deliberate work to maximize your cognitive FITness.

Your thinking patterns profoundly impact your day-to-day experience. Our premise at FIT is that to reach your greatest human potential you must be fully integrated across all life dimensions, requiring time, effort, and intention. Developing Cognitive FITness involves tackling mentally challenging tasks and continuously working to improve and strengthen your habits of thought.

The FIT Assessment provides a snapshot of your cognitive functioning in the areas of Learning, Intellectual Engagement, Attention, Effortful Control, Autonomy, and Social Skills. Receiving feedback regarding these areas of your cognitive functioning allows you to engage in healthier thinking patterns that will cultivate behaviors to achieve your dreams.

Individuals who are cognitively FIT operate in the most optimal mental framework for success. Cognitively FIT habits include productive ways of thinking that are fully integrated with purpose and goals. For instance, cognitive FITness is related to finding solutions to difficult problems. Most importantly, it is crucial to realize that your potential to achieve your wildest dreams begins with you thoughts. We hope you begin your journey to living a Fully Integrated life by engaging with us in the FIT experience.
